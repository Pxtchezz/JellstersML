About Jellsters

Jellsters are an Jester monster that lives in crystal caves. They come in different subtypes, and they can come in any color, pattern, etc. SUBTYPE - All-Seeing Jellsters These Jellsters have eyes all over them (only ONE on the forehead), unlike a default Jellster, that only has an eye on it's chest. These are uncommon to find in the community, and sometimes these types of jellsters creep others out due to their different appearence. SUBTYPE - Hellsters Hellsters are a very dangerous subtype. They're really aggresive and hostile to others. Nothing else to say, but do NOT go near one.
